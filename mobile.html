The Strategic Technical Assistance Response Team (START) Program is part of the DOE Office of Indian Energy effort to assist in the development of tribal renewable energy projects. Through START, Tribes in the 48 contiguous states and Alaska apply for and are selected to receive technical assistance from DOE and national laboratory experts to move projects closer to implementation.
